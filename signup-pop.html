TESTUNE

A Browser based online test making platform for making online Tests, Quizzes, Exams. It is beneficial for Big Educational Institutes, Tuition Centres, Schools and also for Corporates for conducting internal Assessment Tests $ Quizzes. It has varied type of question possibility like Multiple Choice, Paragraph Type, Fill in the Blank etc. The Test Performance is auto evaluated instantly. It can also handle Complex Scientific notations, Diagrams, Chemical and Mathematical Equation etc. It also has a layer for automated delegation of content creation and management of content.
